Console variables for player one:

Q                  -QUIT CONSOLE
A                  -QUIT CONSOLE
GIVE HEALTH(1P)    -+25% HEALTH
GIVE FIVE(1P)      -GIVES 5 GRENADES, 1 AIR STRIKE, 2 TELEPORTS
GIVE FM(1P)        -GIVES 1 FIREBALL MISSILE
GIVE BLOCK(1P)     -GIVES 2 METAL PLATES
GIVE PB(1P)        -GIVES PARACHUTE BOMB
MOVE=0             -SKIP GO
GM_RESTORE         -RESTORES LAND
GM_TOTAL           -RESTORES ALL THINGS
GM_SDEATH          -SUDDEN DEATH MODE
GM_SHOWRESOURCE    -SHOWS RESOURCE(AIR STRIKES,TELEPORTS,GRENADES,F.MISSILES,METAL PLATES,PARACHUTE BOMBS,BLOW SCREEN)
GM_QUIT            -QUITS GAME 
GM_GAMMA           -SETS GAMMA TO 1
GM_WORMGAMMA2      -SETS GAMMA TO 2
GM_ITEMDROP        -ITEMDROP
GM_CURPOS          -SHOWS POSSITION
GM_KILL            -WORM SUICIDES
ON 'D' DROP        -AFTER THAT COMMAND QUIT CONSOLE AND PRESS 'D'(IT'LL BE ITEMDROP)
